## 1. What is incident response?
A structured process used to detect, analyze, contain, and recover from security incidents.

## 2. Phases of incident response?
- Preparation
- Detection
- Containment
- Eradication
- Recovery
- Lessons learned

## 3. Why containment is important?
It prevents the attack from spreading and reduces damage.

## 4. Role of logs in IR?
Logs provide evidence of suspicious activity and help reconstruct the attack timeline.

## 5. What is root cause analysis?
The process of identifying the original reason the incident occurred.
